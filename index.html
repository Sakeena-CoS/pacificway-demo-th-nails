Our Services

From everyday to extraordinary.

T&H Nails offers a full menu of nail services for every style and occasion — gel, acrylic, art, and more.

Gel Manicure
Long-lasting gel colour that stays chip-free for weeks. Choose from a wide range of shades for a flawless, glossy finish that looks salon-fresh every day.
Acrylic Full Set
Strong, sculpted acrylic nails in any shape and length you desire. Our technicians craft beautiful, durable sets that complement your personal style perfectly.
Nail Art & Design
From subtle accents to bold custom artwork — our nail artists create intricate designs, florals, ombre, chrome, and everything in between. Your nails, your canvas.
Pedicure
Treat your feet to a relaxing soak, scrub, and polish. Our pedicure service includes cuticle care, callus treatment, and your choice of classic or gel colour.
Dip Powder
Odourless, long-lasting dip powder nails that strengthen your natural nails while delivering rich, vibrant colour. A healthier alternative to gel and acrylic.
Extensions
Nail extensions that add length and strength for a glamorous look. Choose your shape — square, coffin, stiletto, or almond — and we'll create your dream set.
